JJC Pasig

Our Organization

JJC Pasig is a Non-Governmental Organization that focuses on honing the youth to become leaders of change in the future through intensive training and Nation Building activities. JJC Pasig is a special affiliate of the Junior Chamber International, a youth organization that provides leadership development. We believe that service to humanity is the best work of life.

President Nominee

I am currently the nominee for the position of president. I frequently engage in discussions with the current president and president-elects of our organization, addressing matters related to events, meetings, and all things associated with JJC Pasig. My role involves assisting them in maintaining the active and organized functioning of our organization.

Activities

Our organization prioritizes the welfare of the people, which is why we often engage in distribution activities. Given the current economic challenges in the Philippines, where purchasing items has become increasingly difficult due to high prices, we specifically focus on distributing essentials, particularly rice. We are gradually extending our assistance to all Pasig citizens and expanding our outreach to other cities as part of our ongoing initiatives.
